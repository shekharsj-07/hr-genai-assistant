 The benefits you have include:
1. Health Insurance: Provided to all full-time employees.
2. Employee Assistance Program (EAP) for confidential counseling services.
3. Wellness sessions including yoga, mental health webinars, and stress management.
4. Access to mental health counselors confidentially via the EAP program.
5. Encouragement to take mental health leave when needed, in discussion with HR.
6. L&D: Employees can enroll in up to 2 paid learning programs annually.
7. Every employee has access to a personal development budget of INR 25,000 per year.
8. Employees must complete a minimum of one L&D course every two quarters.
9. Employee Resource Groups (ERGs) are available for support and community engagement.
