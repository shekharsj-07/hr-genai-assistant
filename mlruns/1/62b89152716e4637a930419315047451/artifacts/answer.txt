 The employee benefits, as specified in the ACME CORP HR Policy Handbook, include:

1. Health Insurance: Provided to all full-time employees.
2. L&D (Learning & Development): Employees can enroll in up to 2 paid learning programs annually.
3. Employee Assistance Program (EAP) is available for confidential counseling services.
4. Access to mental health wellness sessions such as yoga, mental health webinars, and stress management.
5. Confidential access to mental health counselors via the EAP program.
6. Employees are encouraged to take mental health leave when needed, in discussion with HR.
7. Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
8. Male employees are entitled to 2 weeks of paternity leave.
